SATALKAR LOTUS HOSPITAL

We are a Superspecialty healthcare group in Urology & Gynecology with the core belief that every life is priceless. We believe in early detection, prevention, and prompt treatment of diseases, to ensure the best chances of recovery at the lowest cost. Satalkar Lotus is a renowned Urologist Hospital in Ahmednagar. It is one of the pioneering health centers in Ahmednagar which serves people as per the guidelines given by the American Urology Association.


Satalkar Lotus Hospital – Urologist Hospital in Ahmednagar began with the intent of helping people recover and lead happy lives. In its 35 yrs of treating many people and saving innumerable lives, the hospital has gained not only the trust but also a need to contact a more extensive populace and help the entire area.

The establisher Dr. Ravindra Satalkar stepped up the establishment stone on which this super specialty hospital claims to fame and stands tall, in his name today.
